Homemade Meatloaf

Description

A recipe for Amish meatloaf topped with bacon strips and a ketchup glaze

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 1/2 cups crushed butter-flavored crackers
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

STEPS

  1. Step 1
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Step 2
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together ground beef, crushed crackers, onion, eggs, 3/4 cup ketchup, and 1/4 cup brown sugar until well blended. Press into a 9x5 inch loaf pan. Lay the two slices of bacon over the top.
  5. Step 3
  6. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until cooked through. While the loaf bakes, mix together the remaining 1 cup ketchup, vinegar, salt, mustard and 1/2 cup brown sugar. Spread over the top of the meat loaf for the last 15 minutes of baking.
